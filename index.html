Almaty, the Cultural Hub

Almaty, Kazakhstan's largest city, offers a vibrant cultural scene with bustling markets, art galleries, and theaters. Enjoy the city's cafes and restaurants, and take a scenic cable car ride to Kok-Tobe Hill for breathtaking views.

Kazakhstan's Silk Road Heritage

Explore Kazakhstan's rich Silk Road history by visiting ancient cities like Turkestan and Taraz. Discover stunning sites such as the Mausoleum of Khoja Ahmed Yasawi and other historical landmarks that reflect the region's cultural exchanges.

Baikonur Cosmodrome

Visit Baikonur Cosmodrome, the world's largest operational space launch facility. Learn about its significant role in space exploration history and witness rocket launches firsthand on guided tours.
